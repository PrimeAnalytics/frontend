We dont compromise on security because we understand the sensitive nature of your company’s data. Amazon Web Services (AWS) sets the industry standard, and we improve on it.

The AWS cloud infrastructure is housed in AWS’s highly secure data centers, which utilize state-of-the art electronic surveillance and multi-factor access control systems. Data centers are staffed 24x7 by trained security guards, and access is authorized strictly on a least privileged basis. All personnel must be screened when leaving areas that contain customer data. The AWS infrastructure is protected by extensive network and security monitoring systems. These systems provide important security measures, such as basic distributed denial of service (DDoS) protection and password brute-force detection on AWS accounts. In addition, AWS infrastructure components are continuously scanned and tested. While some organizations perform vulnerability scanning on their resources once a quarter or once a month, we scan multiple times a day. Not only do they have a large staff of security experts at AWS, but they also have a large set of tools and systems that automate many of their security tasks, both large and small. Everything from managing credentials to monitoring server and network usage, port scanning activities, application usage, and unauthorized intrusion attempts.

The combination of OAuth’s authorisation protocol and Amazon Web Services’ highly secure hosting infrastructure provides the world-class security needed for dealing with your company’s data. You can be rest assured that your data is safe in the cloud.

Our process

1

Client specification

1.
A chat over a cup of tea
As a first step we require our client to give us some examples of the visualizations they would like to see in the specific dashboard. For us this gives an idea of what is important to our client and provides a take-off point from which we can incorporate our own ideas and suggestions. In order to build a powerful and aesthetically pleasing dashboard, we have to conduct some research with the data. This would allow us determine the size and computation capability of the hosting service needed and also the complexity of the data drill-downs which would have to be performed.
2

Develop

2.
Lot's of coffee and a laptop
The next step in our process incorporates the design of the dashboard according to the client’s specifications. Design incorporates not only how your dashboard looks but also how it functions across platforms in the cloud. First we would present options of visual appearance for the client to choose from. Once we have agreed on the appearance we will use the real data from your database to show you examples of what can be done. You will be amazed by the versatility and functionality of your dashboard! Once we have agreed upon the specific visualitaions we will take the process live into the cloud.
3

Implementation

3.
See what we've done
Implementation will occur only after we have fully met our client’s needs and requirements. By now we have worked closely with your team and discussed and adjusted many facets of your personalized dashboard. Making your dashboard accessible over different devices, interactive and in-real-time, would require us to run your data from a centralized server over the internet, otherwise known as ‘the cloud’. In the cloud our software will run computations on your data which will simplify it and make it easy to visualize. This simplified version of your data is then pushed to devices over the internet in order to display beautiful and meaningful visualiizations! Bazinga!
4

Evaluation & Maintenance

4.
Handing over the project
Once we are up and running we invite you to evaluate and criticise our work. For the first month we will make any reasonable changes or adjustments free-of-charge. You don’t always know what you want to know, and we know that! Your data being in the cloud allows us to make adjustments from anywhere in the world. Quality service is our highest priority and we realise it is the defining quality of a successful venture. We will never leave your side!
