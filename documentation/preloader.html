The preloader

Put your spinner into the preloader tag. This can be anything you like, your own spinner, one of the following or one of spinkits other spinners. But if you are not using the sk-spinner, to center your text or alternive spinner, wrapp in tag with a spin class.

http://tobiasahlin.com/spinkit/

Example:

<div class="preloader">
    <div class="spinner"
        Skyline
    </div>
</div><!-- End .preloader -->

To use spinkit:

<div class="preloader">
    <div class="sk-spinner sk-spinner-rotating-plane"></div>
</div><!-- End .preloader -->
